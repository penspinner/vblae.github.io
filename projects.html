Description

Web application design to simulate a stock trading system. Provided managers with the facilities to mange employees, customers, stocks, view revenue, and sales reports. Equipped employees with the tools to mange clients and order in their behalf. Allowed clients to easily buy/sell stocks, keep track of their conditianal trailing-stop and hidden-stop orders, and stock price fluctuations.
